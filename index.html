Abraham Lincoln

A man who saved billion of lives

Here's a timeline of Lincoln's life:

  • 1809 - Born in Hardin County, Kentucky
  • 1816 - Family moves to Indiana
  • 1830 - Family moves to Illinois
  • 1831 - Leaves home and works various jobs in New Salem, Illinois
  • 1832 - Runs for Illinois State Legislature but loses; serves briefly in the Black Hawk War
  • 1834 - Wins election to Illinois State Legislature; begins studying law
  • 1836 - Admitted to the Illinois Bar
  • 1842 - Marries Mary Todd
  • 1846 - Elected to the U.S. House of Representatives
  • 1849 - Returns to Springfield, Illinois to practice law
  • 1858 - Debates Stephen A. Douglas in a series of famous debates while running for the U.S. Senate
  • 1860 - Elected 16th President of the United States
  • 1861 - Inaugurated as President; Civil War begins
  • 1863 - Issues the Emancipation Proclamation
  • 1864 - Re-elected as President
  • 1865 - Delivers his second inaugural address; Civil War ends; Assassinated by John Wilkes Booth at Ford's Theatre on April 14
